$200 million and counting.

September is Childhood Cancer Awareness Month. Since 1998, with support from Hyundai and its dealers, Hyundai Hope On Wheels® has donated $200 million to fund over 1,100 research grants focused on improving outcomes for children battling cancer. With the purchase of any new Hyundai, a portion of the proceeds goes to help Hope On Wheels fight childhood cancer.1 When it comes to fighting childhood cancer, we’re thinking of every mile.

September 9 was World EV Day, a day for the world to reflect on the great strides we're making as we move toward e-mobility. Helping lead the way is the World Electric Vehicle of the Year, the Hyundai IONIQ 5.
